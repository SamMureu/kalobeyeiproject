CHEKECHEA

Our Approach

Chekechea is more than just early learning. It's a comprehensive program that nurtures the whole child - body, mind, and spirit - setting them on a path to thrive.

Program Components

  • Early Childhood Development (ECD) centers in underserved areas
  • Qualified and trained educators
  • Age-appropriate learning materials and activities
  • Focus on cognitive, social, and motor skill development
  • Parent and community engagement

Our Impact

Through Chekechea, we're creating lasting change in some of Kenya's most vulnerable communities. Children who participate in our program are better prepared for primary school and have a stronger foundation for lifelong learning.

How You Can Help

Support our mission to provide quality early childhood education to vulnerable children. Your donation helps us:

  • Build and maintain ECD centers
  • Train and support educators
  • Provide learning materials
  • Engage parents and communities

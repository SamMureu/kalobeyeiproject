ABOUT STILL I RISE

Empowering Society through education, health, and skills training

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower Turkana young minds through education, health, and skills training. We believe that every young person deserves the opportunity to experience a fulfilled life.

Our Vision

We envision a Society where every young person has access to quality education, healthcare, and employment opportunities, breaking the cycle of poverty and inequality.

Our Values

  • Integrity
  • Excellence
  • Passion
  • Accountability
  • Respect

Our History

Founded in 2018, CHEKECHEA has been working tirelessly to create positive change in Turkana communities. Over the years, we have grown from a small organization to a national leader in youth development.
